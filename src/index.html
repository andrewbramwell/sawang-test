Thai Massage

Deeply rooted in the ancient healing traditions of Northern Thailand, my Traditional Thai Massage offers a holistic approach to wellbeing. This therapeutic technique combines gentle stretching, targeted pressure, and energy work to promote deep relaxation, improved flexibility, and balance throughout your body.

Using hands, thumbs, elbows, and sometimes feet, I guide your body through yoga-like stretches while stimulating key pressure points. This authentic method is perfect for alleviating stress, enhancing circulation, and increasing flexibility and range of motion. Each session is performed with you comfortably dressed in provided clothing, allowing for proper movement and stretching.
